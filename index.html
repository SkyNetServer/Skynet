Games on Skynet

SkyWars

Skywars is a multiplayer PvP game where each player starts off on a separate island and tries to battle each other like in PvP. The main goal is to be the last person or team alive to win. Often there is a middle, larger island that contains chests with better loot.

HotBlock

HotBlock is a funny PvP minigame. The goal is to stand as long as possible on the 'Hot Block' in the middle of the arena in order to earn money. Because every player wants to get there, there is an exiting PvP-match. The dangerous poisoned blocks are making the game even more exiting.

Kitpvp

KitPvP is one of the game modes of Minecraft Central. It was added 25th February 2014. The goal of KitPvP is to gain money from kills and with that money upgrade your weaponry and armor to get even better at PvP.
